The Problem
Every student in Dhaka knows
this frustration.
🚌
Zero real-time info
Traffic jam creates uncertainty — students wait at the wrong stop and miss their bus completely.
💺
Can’t check seats
No way to know if seats are available before walking to the bus stop. Students arrive and find the bus full.
💸
Manual cash payment
Cash-only, paper-based system. Slow boarding, untracked revenue, zero receipts for students.
Our Solution
Introducing UniTrack BD
One unified system. Three powerful modules.
📍
Live Bus Tracking
Real-time GPS location of every university bus on an interactive map.
💺
Seat Intelligence
Live seat availability updated in real-time by QR scans and helper board count.
📱
QR Auto-Payment
Students scan QR at boarding — payment auto-deducted, seat count updated instantly.

Feature Deep-Dive
QR Payment Flow in 4 steps
1
Student opens UniTrack app
A unique QR code tied to their student ID and wallet is displayed.
2
Helper scans QR at bus door
Bus helper scans the student’s QR using the helper app. Takes under 2 seconds.
3
Server processes & broadcasts
৳ 15 deducted from wallet. Seat count decrements by 1. All student apps refresh instantly via Socket.io.
4
✅ Payment confirmed
Student gets push notification: “Payment of ৳ 15 confirmed. Enjoy your ride!” Digital receipt saved to history.
