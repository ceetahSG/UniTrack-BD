3-Phase Rollout Strategy
How We Scale From Day 1

Goal: Prove product-market fit. Build on known problem with known audience.

Scope: 14 buses, ~500 daily riders.

Revenue: ৳1.2L/year. Break-even month 3.

Phase 2: University Expansion (Months 4–12)

Goal: Replicate ULAB success across 10+ universities in Dhaka.

Scope: Target NSU, BRAC, DIU, AIUB, IUB, East West, and more. ~150 buses, 10K+ daily riders.

Phase 3: City Scale (Year 2+)

Goal: Partner with BRTC & private operators for Dhaka-wide coverage.

Risk Management
Anticipating Challenges
⚠️ Low student adoption
HIGH

Mitigation: Heavy marketing on campus. Free first month. Incentivize early adopters. Partner with student council to push adoption.

⚠️ GPS reliability in Dhaka
MEDIUM

Mitigation: Fallback to manual helper input. Test with real buses before launch. Use multiple GPS sources. Graceful degradation mode.

⚠️ Payment gateway delays
LOW

Mitigation: Integrate bKash early (month 1). Fallback to manual payment during approval. Pre-agreements with Nagad for Phase 2.

⚠️ University bureaucracy
HIGH

Mitigation: ULAB is our home — we have executive buy-in. Phase 2 universities: partner through student & admin champions. 3–6 month sales cycles planned.
